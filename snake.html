How it Works

The agent doesn't see the whole grid. It only sees:

  • Danger: Is there a wall or tail straight, left, or right?
  • Food Direction: Is food ahead, right, left, or behind (relative to head)?

This simplifies the state space, allowing it to learn very quickly!
